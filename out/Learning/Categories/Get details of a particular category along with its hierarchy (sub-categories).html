GET DETAILS OF A PARTICULAR CATEGORY ALONG WITH ITS HIERARCHY (SUB-CATEGORIES)

Overview

Returns complete information about a category along with its hierarchy (sub-categories) based on the category's ID that is passed as a parameter value.

Requires OAuth

No

Method

GET

URL

https://<hostname-api.sabacloud.com>/v1/learning/category/:id?hierarchy=true
