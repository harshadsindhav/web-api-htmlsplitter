GET DETAILS OF A PARTICULAR FIELD OF STUDY

Overview

Returns complete information about a field of study based on the field of study's ID that is passed as a parameter value.

Requires OAuth

No

Method

GET

URL

https://<hostname-api.sabacloud.com>/v1/fieldofstudy/:id:(:searchFields)
