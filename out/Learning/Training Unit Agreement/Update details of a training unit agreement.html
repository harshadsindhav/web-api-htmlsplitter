UPDATE DETAILS OF A TRAINING UNIT AGREEMENT

Overview

Allows updating an existing training unit agreement based on the Training Unit Agreement's ID.

Requires OAuth

No

Method

PUT

URL

https://<hostname-api.sabacloud.com>/v1/tuagreement/:id

URL (User-friendly)
You can use a user-friendly URL which accepts the agreement_no instead of the internal TrainingUnitAgreement's ID.

https://<hostname-api.sabacloud.com>/v1/tuagreement/agreement_no%3D<TrainingUnitAgreementsNo>
